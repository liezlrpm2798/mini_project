Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


I am a First-time client/patient. What service should I book?

For clients/patients who are having their first session or who do not know what service to book, you may choose to have a Mental Health Consultation as this is the initial/intake session that we recommend to our clients/patients.

How do I book a session?

You may book a session by clicking on the Make Appointment button below on the upper right corner of your screen. You may also book a session by visiting www.thrivingminds.com/appointment or by messaging or calling at +63 917 777 2888 (Globe and TM) or (02) 8888 - 1111 (Landline)

Who will be helping me?

Different therapists have different approaches and areas of focus, so it's important to find the right person who can achieve the best results for you. We have found that we are able to provide a successful match most of the time; however, if you start the process and you feel your therapist isn't a good fit for you, you may elect to be matched to a different therapist.

How much does it cost?

The cost of consultation through Thriving minds ranges from Php 700 to Php 1,200 and it is based on your location, preferences, and therapist availability.



If you are in a crisis or any other person may be in danger - don't use this site. These resources can provide you with immediate help.
